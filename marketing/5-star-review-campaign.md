# 5-Star Review Promotion Campaign

## Goal
50+ reviews per listing, 4.8+ average rating

---

## When to Ask

| Timing | Method | Script |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Same day (evening) | SMS | Short, warm |
| Day 3 | Email | With photo of their lawn |
| Day 7 | SMS | Final ask |

**Only ask after pulse check score is 4 or 5**

---

## Incentive Ideas (Compliant)

**Cannot offer incentives for positive reviews specifically.**

Compliant options:
- "Leave a review (any rating) for a chance to win $100 gift card"
- Monthly drawing for all reviewers
- Donation to local charity for each review

---

## Review Velocity Targets

| Timeframe | Reviews | Cumulative |
|-----------|---------|------------|
| Month 1 | 5 | 5 |
| Month 2 | 8 | 13 |
| Month 3 | 10 | 23 |
| Month 6 | 15/mo | 50+ |
| Month 12 | 15/mo | 100+ |

---

## Handling No Response

If customer doesn't leave review after 3 asks:
- Stop asking
- They may be satisfied but not review-givers
- Focus energy on next customer

Never:
- Pester repeatedly
- Offer money for reviews
- Create fake reviews

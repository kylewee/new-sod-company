## Negative Review Handling

### Step 1: Don't Panic
- Take a breath before responding
- Never respond emotionally

### Step 2: Investigate
- Pull up customer record
- Talk to crew that did the job
- Understand what happened

### Step 3: Respond Publicly
- Acknowledge the issue
- Apologize for their experience
- Offer to resolve offline

### Step 4: Resolve Privately
- Call the customer directly
- Listen to their concerns
- Offer appropriate resolution

### Step 5: Follow Up
- After resolution, politely ask if they'd update review
- Never demand or pressure
- Some customers will, some won't

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Google Business Profile
- Most important platform for local SEO
- Respond to ALL reviews
- Add photos regularly
- Post updates weekly

### Yelp
- Claim and verify listing
- Respond to reviews
- Don't ask for Yelp reviews directly (TOS)
- Let customers find it organically

### Facebook
- Enable recommendations
- Respond to all feedback
- Share positive reviews as posts

### BBB
- Maintain A+ rating
- Respond to complaints within 14 days
- Complete profile with all services
